## The basic idea

The proposal is to consider a multimodal trail as a public-benefit complement alongside the broader Alto corridor concept where appropriate. The emphasis is on adding community, access, and placemaking value rather than treating the corridor only as a transportation asset.

## What this page is for

This page should help visitors answer three questions quickly:

- What is the trail concept?
- How does it relate to Alto?
- What is not being claimed?

## What this is not

This site is not presenting a finished engineering design. It is not claiming that every corridor segment is suitable, that all constraints are resolved, or that a trail automatically addresses every concern associated with a major infrastructure project.

## Why clarity matters

Public discussion improves when the concept is described precisely. That helps decision-makers, technical readers, communities, and skeptics evaluate the idea on its merits rather than on assumptions.
