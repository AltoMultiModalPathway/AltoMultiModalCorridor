## Public value beyond transportation

Large corridor projects influence more than travel times. They also shape access, communities, public space, and long-term regional value. A multimodal trail is worth considering because it could help extend the corridor’s public benefit beyond rail users alone.

## What matters to communities

For corridor communities, the key questions are practical:

- Does this improve access?
- Does it add quality-of-life value?
- Could it support recreation, active transportation, or local vitality?
- Does it help create a more publicly legible benefit from the corridor?
