## Why the timeline matters

Complex projects are easier to understand when readers can see milestones in sequence. This page should become the site’s dated orientation layer.

## MVP structure

The timeline can grow over time, but the page should eventually provide:

1. a milestone date
2. what happened
3. why it matters
4. a source or reference where appropriate

## Initial direction

Use this page to establish that the site is active and current even before there is a full update/news workflow.
