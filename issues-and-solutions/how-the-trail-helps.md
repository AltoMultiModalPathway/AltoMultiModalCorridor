## A disciplined argument

This page should avoid hype. The goal is not to claim that the trail solves everything. The goal is to show where it may help and to say plainly where it does not resolve the underlying issue.

## Suggested structure

For each major concern area, this page should eventually answer:

- What is the concern?
- How might the trail help?
- What are the limitations?
- What still needs study?

## Why this helps the site

This page connects the persuasive case to the site’s proof layer. It is one of the most important pages for decision-makers, skeptical readers, and technically minded visitors.
