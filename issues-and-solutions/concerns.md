## Why this page matters

This page should build trust by showing that concerns are being taken seriously. It should not rush into rebuttal.

## Common concern categories

- community disruption
- access and interface concerns
- land-use or property concerns
- safety and operations concerns
- environmental concerns
- cost and feasibility concerns

## Content intent

This page is meant to summarize concern categories in plain language, show that they are legitimate subjects for public discussion, and prepare readers for a more precise page on how the trail concept may help in some areas.
