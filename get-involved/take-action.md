## MVP action model

This page should start with the lowest-friction actions and grow over time.

### Immediate actions

- read the core case pages
- review the concerns and evidence pages
- share the site with interested community members or partner organizations
- follow timeline updates as the campaign develops

### Later action pathways

As the campaign matures, this page can point to:

- contact decision-makers
- attend public events
- sign up for updates
- use downloadable briefing materials
