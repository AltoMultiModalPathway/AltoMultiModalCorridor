## What belongs here

This page is the main proof layer for the MVP. It should separate:

- verified factual context
- evidence-backed interpretation
- open questions
- advocacy conclusions

## Research content goals

- summarize authoritative background on Alto and the relevant corridor context
- capture evidence relevant to multimodal and public-benefit arguments
- link clearly to underlying sources
- date time-sensitive material

## MVP note

This initial implementation provides the structure and page flow. The detailed evidence summaries and citations should be added as the research set is assembled and verified.
